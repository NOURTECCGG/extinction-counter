About This Project

This website exists as a quiet reflection on extinction. It is not an official scientific dashboard, but a symbolic representation based on real global estimates.

Species loss is often invisible in daily life. This project attempts to make that invisibility visible, without noise, pressure, or distraction.

The counter is based on public biodiversity research from organizations such as IUCN and IPBES. It represents an estimate — not an exact measurement.

The goal is not to alarm, but to invite attention.
